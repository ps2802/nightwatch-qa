# Operating Principles

1. **GitHub or it didn’t happen.** No Docs/Notion secrets. Every decision, report, and workflow belongs in this repo.
2. **Open-source-first stack.** Playwright, mdBook, Pandoc, Invoice Ninja, MinIO, and similar tools lead. SaaS add-ons are optional upgrades and must be justified in `/playbooks/tooling.md` if introduced.
3. **Overnight delivery.** Inputs by 20:00 local, outputs by 08:00. If SLA slips, log it in `/ops/slas.md`.
4. **Specialized agents.** Atlas (CEO), Relay (PM), Scout (research), Spin (test runner), Outline (UX critic), Echo (copy critic), Funnel (conversion), Lens (report), Pulse (sales/follow-up), Ledger (finance/ops).
5. **Paperclip is auxiliary only.** Use it for reminders, escalations, and org graph—not for storing deliverables.
6. **Hard rule:** No new infrastructure unless it directly improves first revenue, delivery quality, or reviewability.
7. **Fit scoring precedes outreach.** Prospects must be logged in `/ops/prospect-fit/prospects.csv` with scoring fields filled before contact.
8. **Evidence or it’s opinion.** Screenshots, HAR files, or Loom clips accompany every finding.
9. **Client-ready from GitHub.** Reports must be reviewable straight from Markdown/PDF committed here.
10. **Voice + accountability.** Updates follow the Rocky format: what happened, what’s next, what’s blocked, what’s needed.
